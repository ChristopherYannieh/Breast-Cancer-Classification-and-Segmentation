Breast Cancer Classification and Segmentation

Breast Cancer, the second-leading cause of cancer deaths in women, is the disease women fear most.
Breast cancer can also occur in men, but it is far less common than in women.
With the advance of technology and machinery, the fear of this disease is becoming less, but it doesn't lessen it's danger.
This website is used to segment tumor, if present, in an ultrasound and classify it as benign or malignant.
Click on "Choose file", to be able to upload the ultrasound image, then click on "Submit", to be able to process the photo.
